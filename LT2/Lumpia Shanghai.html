Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilogram ground pork
  • 1/2 cup singkamas, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup carrots, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup green onions, finely chopped
  • 1/8 cup celery (equiv. to 2 Tbsp)
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 2 tsps sesame oil
  • lumpia wrappers
  • cooking oil for deep frying

    Instructions

  2. Spoon about 1 tablespoon of the mixture into each lumpia wrapper. Roll tightly. Seal ends of the wrapper with water.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown. Serve hot.
