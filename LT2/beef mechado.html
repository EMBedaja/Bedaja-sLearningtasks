Beef Mechado

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/2 large red onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 kilo beef,(use brisket), cut into large chunks
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • 3 potatoes, quarted
  • 1 carrot, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 2 bell pepper, sliced
  • salt, to taste

    Instructions

  1. Heat cooking oil in a pot then saut√© the onion and garlic.
  2. Pour in the tomato sauce, soy sauce, beef stock and let it boil for a few minutes.
  3. Cover and continue to simmer everything over low heat until the beef is tender, approximately 1 hour. Check the pot from time to time if you need to add more stock or water.
  4. Once the beef is tender, add in the potatoes, carrots and cook for about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the bell pepper and cook for about 5 minutes just before serving.
