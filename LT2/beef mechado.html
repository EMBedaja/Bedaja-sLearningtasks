Beef Mechado

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/2 large red onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 kilo beef,(use brisket), cut into large chunks
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • 3 potatoes, quarted
  • 1 carrot, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 2 bell pepper, sliced
  • salt, to taste

    Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat the cooking oil and then saut√© the onion and garlic.
  2. Cover and continue to cook over low heat for about 1 hour, or until the beef is cooked. If you need to add more stock or water, check the pot from time to time.
  3. Add the potatoes and carrots once the steak is cooked and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  4. Just before serving, add the bell pepper and simmer for about 5 minutes.
