Buko Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 packs NESTLÉ All Purpose Cream 250ml
  • 1/2 cup Nestle Condensada
  • 1 800g fruit cocktail, drained
  • 5 pcs young coconut, grated and drained
  • 1 cup green or red kaong, drained
  • 1 cup Nata de Coco, drained

    Instructions

  1. Combine NESTLÉ All Purpose Cream and Nestle Condensada in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Toss in the rest of the ingredients with the prepared cream-milk mixture and mix very well to fully incorporate.
  3. Chill it 45 minutes.
  4. Serve, share and enjoy!
