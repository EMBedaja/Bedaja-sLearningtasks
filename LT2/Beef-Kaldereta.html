Beef-Kaldereta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef short ribs ,cut into pieces, blanched
  • 2 pcs bay leaf
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 pc potato, cubed
  • 1 pc small carrot, cubed
  • 1 pc onion, chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pc red chili, sliced
  • ½ cup tomato paste
  • 1 small can liver spread
  • 1 pc red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 pc green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 sachets MAGGI MAGIC SARAP 8g
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar

    Instructions

  1. Cook the meat in a pot of water with bay leaves until it is tender. Remove the beef from the soup and set it aside.(2.5hours)
  2. In a pan sauté the potato and carrot until golden brown. Set aside after straining. (10mins)
  3. In the same pan, sauté the garlic, onion, and chile. Toss in the tomato paste and the liver spread. Cook for another 2 minutes. Pour in the broth that has been set aside. Add the beef, carrots, and bell peppers that have been sautéed. Cook for 15 minutes on low heat.(20mins)
  4. Season with sugar and MAGGI MAGIC SARAP. Serve immediately on a serving dish. (10mins)
