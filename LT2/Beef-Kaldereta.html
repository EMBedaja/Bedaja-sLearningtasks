Beef-Kaldereta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef short ribs ,cut into pieces, blanched
  • 2 pcs bay leaf
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 pc potato, cubed
  • 1 pc small carrot, cubed
  • 1 pc onion, chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pc red chili, sliced
  • ½ cup tomato paste
  • 1 small can liver spread
  • 1 pc red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 pc green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 sachets MAGGI MAGIC SARAP 8g
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar

    Instructions

  1. Simmer beef in water with bay leaves until tender. Strain beef and reserve broth. (2.5hours)
  2. Sauté potato and carrot in oil until golden brown. Strain and set aside. (10mins)
  3. Sauté garlic, onion and chili in the same pan. Add tomato paste and liver spread. Cook for 2 minute. Stir in reserved broth. Add beef, sautéed potato carrot and bell peppers. Simmer for 15 minutes. (20mins)
  4. Season with MAGGI MAGIC SARAP and sugar. Transfer on a serving plate and serve. (10mins)
