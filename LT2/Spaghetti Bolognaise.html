Simple Spaghetti Bolognaise Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion finely chopped
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 good sized handful fresh parsley chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ pound spaghetti
  • Grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese for serving optional

    Methods

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the onions for 2 to 3 minutes, or until they are gently brown. Fry for another minute after adding the garlic.

  2. Fry the ground beef until it is fully cooked and no pink remains in the meat (about 8 minutes). Combine the oregano and tomato paste with the meat and stir well.

  3. Bring the tomatoes and parsley to a boil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Reduce to a low heat and continue to cook for at least 15 minutes (you can cook longer for a deeper flavor).

  4. Cook the spaghetti according to the package directions in the meantime. Drain and serve with the sauce and cheese on top (if using).
