Egg fried rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • Egg
  • Cold rice
  • Salt or/and soy sauce
  • Green onion, optional

    Method

  1. Slice green onions and beat eggs well with a touch of salt. Set aside.

  2. Heat a wok or a skillet over high heat; add 1 tbsp cooking oil and swirl to coat. Pour beaten eggs into the wok and scramble.

  3. Add the remaining 2 tbsp frying oil and the cold jasmine rice after the eggs are cooked 3/4 way.

  4. Breakdown rice and mix it with the eggs.

  5. Add soy sauce to the wok's edge, allowing it to burn slightly for a smokey umami taste. Season with salt to taste and thoroughly combine all ingredients. If necessary, lower the heat to a low setting.

  6. Turn off the heat, stir in green onions. Enjoy with any other dishes you decided to serve with!
