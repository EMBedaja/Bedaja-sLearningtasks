     KNOW YOUR RIGHTS      
LGBT Actions    

  • PROMOTE LEGAL PROTECTIONS

Only 11 countries in the world have constitutions that protect people from discrimination based on their sexual orientation. We should assist people in overturning discriminatory legislation and enshrining LGBTQ rights in law.

  • CHAMPIONING ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT

It has been established that greater economic inclusiveness benefits emerging economies. We should support in ensuring that LGBTQ persons have access to the resources and rights they require to succeed in the workplace.

  • BUILDING COMMUNITY SUPPORT

Despite significant advances, there remains a big global difference on LGBTQ rights. We help others in providing knowledge and raising awareness in order to foster good, accepting communities.

  • ENSURING ACCESS TO HEALTH AND SAFETY

LGBTQ people are often persecuted, and their lives and livelihoods are harmed. We should provide health care and safe sanctuary to LGBTQ people who are vulnerable to persecution.

  • PROVIDING FREE LEGAL AID

LGBTQ people still lack proper access to justice in many countries. We should support in providing pro bono legal assistance to LGBTQ individuals who are facing discriminatory legal action.

  • DEFENDING THE RIGHT TO LOVE

LGBTQ people have the right to love and to choose who they want to spend their life with because they also have human rights like us.
