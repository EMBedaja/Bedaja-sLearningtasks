Cucumber Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • cucumber
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Honey
  • Fresh parsley and dill
  • Fresh garlic
  • Red onion

    Methods

  1. Cucumbers, salted and drained: place sliced cucumbers in a colander.

  2. Sprinkle with salt and toss to coat thoroughly, separating slices as needed to ensure that salt is properly distributed.

  3. Allow cucumbers to drain for 30â€“60 minutes in a colander put over a dish in the refrigerator.

  4. Meanwhile, combine apple cider vinegar, olive oil, mayonnaise, honey, parsley, dill, garlic, and pepper in a mixing bowl until emulsified (well blended).

  5. Dry cucumbers, spread cucumbers over a double layer of paper towels.

  6. Toss everything together in a large mixing bowl with the cucumbers and red onion. Once the dressing has blended, pour it over the cucumbers. Toss well to coat. Within one hour, serve.
