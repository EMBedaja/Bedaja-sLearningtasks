OUR APPROACH

PROTECT PEOPLE'S RIGHTS

GIVING GENERAL SUPPORT GRANTS
that empower LGBTQ organizations to build their own ideas for attaining equality and justice while being agile and resilient.

TAKING SMART RISKS
on up-and-coming grassroots groups and leaders. Some of our grantees have gone on to become nationally and globally known campaigners despite never having received outside assistance prior to our financing.

CONNECTING GRANTEES
to exchange information about LGBTQ rights with one another and with professionals, and to develop adaptable solutions that can be expanded locally or internationally.

FOSTERING ORGANIZATIONS’ SUSTAINABILITY
by supporting LGBT individuals where they are now and in the future, as well as providing emergency assistance when necessary.

EVALUATING OUR IMPACT
by analyzing the significant difference that is being made in communities throughout the world and suggesting opportunities for improvement.
